How Our breakthrough Credit Repair Works

Let's work you through the steps

Step 1: The Credit Audit

Timeframe: 1 to 2 Business Days

We review your credit report from top to bottom to identify errors such as incorrect account codes, inaccurate dates, or even accounts that just don’t belong. During your audit we will also establish a plan to help you improve your credit with your balances, and credit mix.

Step 2: Dispute Process

Timeframe: 40 to 60 Days Each Round

By leveraging the Fair Credit Reporting Act, Fair Debt Collections Practices Act, Fair Credit Billing Act, and other consumer protection laws we investigate every derogatory account on your credit. During the Dispute Process we work to remove these accounts that are holding back your credit scores.

Step 3: Dispute Results

Timeframe: 30 to 45 days

Every month we review your credit monitoring reports to see what accounts have been removed, how the bureaus responded, and if there is any new information reporting. These results are logged into your online portal and you are notified immediately of your results!

Step 4: Re-Investigation

Timeframe: Depends on Credit History

When items on your credit report still have errors reporting we escalate the dispute process to challenge the items at a deeper level. These disputes are specific to the responses from the previous disputes and are very aggressive.

Step 5: Graduation

Timeframe: Depends on your credit history.

After we have completed the dispute process in it’s entirety you are ready to graduate our services! This is an exciting time for us and you. Your scores should be soaring by now and you will be ready to take the next step in achieving your dreams. You will receive a completion certificate email.
